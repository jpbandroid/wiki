Windivs 2k3

Windivs 2k3 is the 1st major version of Windivs, released to manufacturing (RTM) on July 23, 2023. The first preview was released to the Technical Preview program on July 15, 2023. Service Pack 1 released to RTM on July 29, 2023 and Service Pack 2 released to RTM on October 5, 2023.
