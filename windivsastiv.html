Windivs 2k8

Windivs 2k8 (internally and occasionally publicly referred to as Windivs Astiv) is the 2nd major version of Windivs, released to manufacturing (RTM) on October 5, 2023. The first preview was released to the Technical Preview program on July 19, 2023. Major new features include a new theme, Slate+, which is a slightly modified version of the Lautus theme from ReactOS made to match Windows Vista's design aesthetics more closely, a Disk Cleanup applet, shutdown sound support, renaming Windivs Workstation to Windivs Professional, and some small screensaver improvements.
